Why it exists

Three things, done right

Case review shouldn't be slow, error-prone, or soul-draining. The Assistant tackles all three at once.

🎯

Accuracy first

Every field is auto-extracted and cross-checked — owner, age, GCI freshness, phone logs, Sherlock handling and sentiment — with manual-verify flags surfaced so nothing slips through.

⚡

Speed by design

One click loads the panel, fills the Estarta template, and a bulk queue auto-advances through dozens of SRs. What took minutes now takes seconds.

🎉

Fun on purpose

Themes, particle effects, confetti, achievements and a team leaderboard turn a repetitive task into something you actually look forward to.

Capabilities

Everything in one panel

🔍

Smart Extraction

Auto-pulls SR number, case age, owner, owner-since date, and last engineer activity the moment a case loads.

🩺

Case Health Score

A 0–100 score with an A–F grade, broken down across GCI freshness, phone log, sentiment, activity, and age.

🔮

Sherlock Detection

Detects Sherlock handling, transfer & requeue messages, and surfaces the AI's reasoning for escalation review.

😊

Sentiment Analysis

Reads prior reviews to gauge sentiment, with a trend sparkline and the age->5 Positive/Negative rule enforced.

📞

Phone Log Check

Verifies a phone log was added within 3 days of ownership, with per-owner timestamps and a diagnose tool.

📋

GCI Status

Checks each GCI field's freshness against the 3-day threshold and flags when an update is needed.

⚡

Bulk Review Queue

Paste SRs from Excel, then glide through with auto-advance and one-click "Open" links that auto-load each panel.

📝

Auto Form Filler

Carries every field over to the Estarta review template and fills it in for you — just verify and submit.

🏆

Team Leaderboard

Today / Week / Month / Total standings synced across the team, with medals and your row highlighted.

🎖️

Achievements

Unlock 19+ badges — streaks, daily milestones, all-clear cases, and a hidden secret to discover.

📜

Review History

A timeline merging reviews posted on the case with ones you logged here, complete with grades and authors.

🎨

Living Themes

Midnight starlight, Cyberpunk rain, Sakura petals, Aurora bands, Royal gold dust & more — each with its own animated particle effect.

📊

Personal Stats

Track today, this week, this month and all-time reviews, deduped once per case per day.

💬

Status Templates

Auto-detects the latest Current Status from prior reviews and offers a dozen quick-pick templates.

👋

Smart Greetings

Time-of-day aware welcome banners that keep the grind a little more human.

🎉

All-Clear Celebration

Confetti fires when a case has zero flags to verify — clean work deserves a little flair.

🌈

Rainbow Names

A playful animated username for select users — a little reward for the legends.

🚀

Zero Setup

Install once and the launcher appears next to the SR — click it and the panel loads automatically.

Get started

Up and running in 4 steps

1️⃣

Request Access

The script is licensed for authorized users only. Reach out on Webex to odemar@cisco.com to get your username added to the access list.

2️⃣

Install Tampermonkey

Add the Tampermonkey extension to your browser from its official store.

3️⃣

Click Install Loader

Hit the button below — Tampermonkey will open a one-click install prompt automatically.

4️⃣

Open a Case

Confirm the install, then open any supported case. The red launcher appears next to the SR — click it and the panel loads.
